Meet Our Managing Director

Mr. DANIEL ANTHONY OKELEKE

Anthony holds a Bachelors degree in Accounting degree from Lagos State University and Membership of Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria. A seasoned Investment Banker with vast experience in Assets management, Portfolio Classification and management, Anthony excelled as an Investment Analyst with broad insight in Shares Securitisation and Collaterization. Reputed for Idea Generation, Research and Analysis, Anthony is exceptional in Deals Origination, Facilitation & Execution and was involved in many large-scale transactions both in the Regular and Over The Counters (OTC) Market. .

As an Investment Executive and Liason Person, he was responsible for High Net-worth Individuals, Institutional Investors & Regulatory Authorities. He Played key roles in various Mergers & Acquisition transactions, Initial Public Offerings (IPOs), and private placement for notable Blue chips and conglomerates. He handled sensitive Portfolios, Pension Funds Accounts and was liaison Executive for Market Regulatory Authorities.
Anthony Ventured into corporate entrepreneurship with focus on Industrial growth, Regional Economic Integration and Intra-African Trade development. He is the MD/CEO of Elyon Kingdom Investments Ltd, a Quality Assurance organisation with Investment Banking background, and Executive Vice Chairman of Ahvalon Executive Ferry Services ltd.
