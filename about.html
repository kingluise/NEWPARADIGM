Who We Are

New-Paradign Investment Chanel(NPIC) is a Media Organisation premised on Investment Broadcast, Finacial information research and Capital Market analysis. An investment media covering multiple spheres of investing, NPIC is providing round the clock Researched Financial information and investment opportunities served in a differentiated manner for inclusivity. We are pioneering an informative, interactive and analysis based Media platform "The Investment Planet " for strategic interactions, opinion mining and research based analysis focused on Capital market advancement. "The Investment Planet" is an Enlightenment Advisory & Advocacy Platform where Market Stakeholders and Seasoned Industry Analysts interface with the investing public to Broaden the reach & Depth of the Capital Market.

The Platform seeks to leverage the strength of the Capital Market and optimise its potentials as catalyst for Industrial Growth. It focuses on Medium Scale Enterprises (MSE'S), the critical component of every economic structure that provides employment opportunities, contributing to GDP and fostering innovation. MSE'S increase economic diversification and boost resilience, neglecting these Economic Growth Agents usually comes at a huge cost. NPIC is innovating ways of providing MSE'S with capital, removing the bureaucratic bottlenecks and reducing the distance, time and cost to Capital.

The Investment Planet platform represents a significant advancement in capital raising process for new Issuers and provision of secondary liquidity for quoted companies, setting a new standard for industrial sector funding and growth. ☆☆☆ By bridging the access gap between Nigerian enterprises and the markets, we are laying the foundation for a stronger economic growth, investor participation, and long-term value creation”.

The Investment Planet Team is Driven by the quest to attract younger generation of investors, Promote Investment Culture & Discipline across the various strata of our society. We are focused on exposing the Public to investment opportunities, availing investors tools that enhance financial literacy and skills to maximise returns on investment We have initiated coordinated efforts to deepen financial inclusion by onboarding multiple millions of unbanked and investment-excluded Nigerians into the capital market. In simplifying access to Capital, investment channels & empowering individuals with knowledge and confidence, we are essentially building the stage for a more inclusive and resilient economy.

NPIC envisions a Capital Market influenced by efficient regulation, Robust Collaborations and a strong Sense of Community. A market Programmed to become a sensation among African Markets and qualified to join the exalted league of developed Capital Market globally

Our Objectives

Investment Planet seeks to Nexus Market Stakeholders for Capital market centered discussions, ☆☆☆☆public enlightenment, interactive programs & activities focused on market development & growth. The goal is to ensure the Investing Public, Industrial Sector and the Economy benefit from the opportunities and potentials of the Capital Market.
The Investment Planet has four Braod objectives:
1. Provide the Platform where Regulators, Operators and Market analysts interface with the Investing Public .
2. Broaden the Reach and Depth of the Market in collaboration with Market Stakeholders.
3. Identify Medium Scale Enterprises with growth potential and Assist in accessing the Market for Capital.
4. Leverage the Capital Market as catalyst for Industrial Growth & Economic Development.


The focus includes to Frame a positive image about the Market, circulate information that Stimulate positive sentiments and push the narratives that will help shape investors perception.
